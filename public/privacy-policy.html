Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how GPT-to-Calendar ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service to create and manage calendar events.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • Event details (title, description, date, time, location)
  • Calendar metadata (calendar ID)
  • Time zone information

We do not collect personal identification information beyond what is provided in the event details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Create events in Google Calendar
  • Retrieve and display calendar events
  • Improve our service functionality

Data Storage and Security

All calendar operations are performed using a secure service account. We do not permanently store your event data on our servers. Event information is only temporarily stored for processing purposes and is automatically deleted after the event is created.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Calendar API to create and manage calendar events. Your use of this service is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
